About Me

Research Scientist & Human Factors Engineer

I am a Human Factors and UX Researcher with 8+ years of experience spanning usability evaluation, human performance research, and mixed-methods analysis across aviation, healthcare, industrial safety, and robotics.

I design and execute end-to-end human-subject studies — from IRB protocol development and participant recruitment through physiological data collection (Tobii eye-tracking, EMG, EDA, heart rate, motion capture) and stakeholder-ready synthesis. I apply statistical modeling and data science workflows in Python and R to translate behavioral signals into actionable, defensible findings for regulated, high-reliability environments.

Currently serving as Human Factors Researcher II at the FAA via Cherokee Federal, delivering human-in-the-loop insights guiding automation and interface design decisions. Recipient of the AAUW National Fellowship, HFES Dieter W. Jahns Award, and the Mark Diamond Research Grant.
